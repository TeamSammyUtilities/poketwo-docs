# Pokétwo Wiki
Pokétwo is a discord bot that allow players to catch Pokémon within discord

## Gameplay 
• Catch Pokémon <br>
<small> • For full guide visit [Catching]({{ sitebase }}catching.md)</small>
Chat to trigger wild Pokémon spawns and outrace your friends to catch them first!

Grow your collection, complete your Pokédex, and show off your rare Pokémon like [Shinies], or Rares.

• Trade and Sell Pokémon <br>
<small> • For the full guide visit [Trading]</small>
Earn Pokécoin by trading with others, sell your Pokémon through the [market], or [auction] them.

• Battle with other Trainers <br>
<small> • For the full guide visit [Battling]</small>
Battle trainers in strategic duels, level up your team, change Pokémon form, or join other server that has [gym].
## History
Pokétwo was created back in May 30, 2020 by Oliver Ni as the successor to Pokécord. Unlike its predecessor, Pokétwo was built as a fully open-source project. 

In 2022 during the slash command migration, Pokétwo transitioned from the `p!` prefix to `@Pokétwo` mention prefix following Discord's strict Message Content Intent policy, which restricted bots from reading raw text.
