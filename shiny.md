# Shiny
Shiny Pokémon are Pokémon with alternative colors. When you catch a Pokémon, there is a 1 in 4096 chance of being a shiny. Shinies does not appear on the spawn image, only on catch.

Buying a Shiny Charm from the shop, will increase the shiny chance of all pokémon by 20% (1 in 3143) for a week. Shiny Charms does NOT stack.

## Shiny Hunting
Shiny hunting will increase the chance of catching one, by ~x1.05, this value will decrease as your shiny streak grows. You can shiny hunt one specific species of Pokémon, so shiny hunting another one will reset your current streak. To shiny hunt a Pokémon use the command `@Pokétwo#8236 shinyhunt/sh <Pokémon>`.

### Formula
The formula used for shiny hunting is referred below:

$$ \left(1 + \frac{\sqrt{\text{streak}}}{7}\right) \times \frac{1}{4096} \times 100 $$

### Shiny Hunting Regional
You cannot shiny hunt a regional or a variant of Pokémon. However, you may only catch the specific Pokémon variant you are looking for.

## Uses
Shiny Pokémon tends to sell to a higher price in the [Market] or the [Auction], price of shiny are deterministic and is always changing due to other factors.
